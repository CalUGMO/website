Thugmo

Thugmo is the Men’s Ultimate B Team for the University of California, Berkeley. It hopes to develop future Ultimate stars who can go onto play for our A Team, UGMO, as well as provide a place for people that want to play some club Ultimate and develop their skills.

Thugmo is the current Southwest Developmental College Men’s reigning champ and looks to continue the tradition. Thugmo was ranked 184(including all the A teams) in the Nation last year and is looking to break into the top 150 this season with the help of captains: Logan Ritter, Ayush Sharma, Kevin Gibson Weinberger

Contact

Thugmo does most of its new player recruitment in the Fall, but will accept new players in the Spring if they possess enough Ultimate experience. For any questions feel free to reach out at calmensultimateB@gmail.com
