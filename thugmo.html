Thugmo

Pictured above is some of our team visiting current player, Sohum Hulyalkar’s, alma mater, Los Gatos High School.


Thugmo is the Men’s Ultimate B Team for the University of California, Berkeley. It hopes to develop future Ultimate stars who can go onto play for our A Team, UGMO, as well as provide a place for people that want to play some club Ultimate and develop their skills.

Thugmo is the current Southwest Developmental College Men’s reigning champ and looks to continue the tradition. Thugmo was ranked 226th (including all the A teams) in the Nation last year and is looking to break into the top 150 this season with the help of captains: David E. Campos, Logan E. Ritter, and John S. Diven.

Contact

Thugmo does most of its new player recruitment in the Spring, but will accept new players in the Fall if they possess enough Ultimate experience. For any questions feel free to reach out at calbutlimate@gmail.com.
